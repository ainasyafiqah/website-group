Prevention from Covid-19

Ways of Prevention

20 January 2022 / Wear a Mask Why we need to wear mask to prevent from Covid-19 infection?

Mask helps on making barrier to prevent someone to catch or spread the virus

20 January 2022 / Avoid Places with big crowd Why we need to avoid places that have big crowd?

The potential of the virus spreading among the crowd is high because we cannot see the virus with our bare eyes.

20 January 2022 / Wash hand Is washing hands important?

We touch everything using our & we did not want any of the virus on our hand. It's best to bring hand sanitizer everytime going out.

20 January 2022 / Monitor Your Health Frequently Why need to monitor our health condition?

To make sure antibody is strong from catching the virus & doesn't have health problems that will worsen health condition.

20 January 2022 / Self Quarantine at Home Why we need to self quarantine?

If you have any of the infected symtoms it's best to self quarantine to prevent from catching and spreading the virus.

20 January 2022 / Social Life Why we need to be aware with socializing?

When you are outdoor, it's best to stand 2meter from other people, it's to make sure that the virus can't spread to you.
